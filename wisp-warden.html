Wisp Warden — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

Wisp Warden ("the app") is developed and published by Matthew Dent ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we collect

The current version of Wisp Warden collects no personal data. Your game progress (best time, coins, upgrades, settings) is stored only on your device using local storage. It is never transmitted to us or to any third party, and deleting the app deletes it.

Advertising and purchases

This version of the app contains no advertising SDK and no active in-app billing. If a future update adds advertising (e.g. Google AdMob) or an in-app purchase (Google Play Billing), this policy and the Play Store Data Safety section will be updated first, and any ad partner's data collection (such as the Android Advertising ID) will be disclosed here.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.
